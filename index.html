Deborah Sussman

Deborah Sussman was an Environmental Graphic Designer from Brooklyn, New York. She was a pioneer the field of graphic design and and has been internationally applauded for her contributions. Prior to starting her own business, she gaind experience in the field by working on exhibits for IBM and the Government of India. Soona fter establishing herself as Deborah Sussman & Company, she met her husband Paul Prejza, an urban planner and architect. Onced married, they joined forces and the office was renamed Sussman/Prejza & Company.
